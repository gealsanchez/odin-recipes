Rich Slow Cooker Hot Chocolate

Description

Hosting a party? Try this slow-cooker hot chocolate for an easy way to serve a crowd. You could also add your favourite liqueur to each mug

Ingredients

  • 6 cups milk
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 ½ cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine milk, condensed milk, chocolate chips, heavy cream, and vanilla extract in a slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on Low, stirring occasionally, until chocolate chips are melted and mixture is smooth, about 2 hours.
